Hosting the Ultimate Disney Plus Watch Party: A Complete Guide

If you're still getting familiar with hosting a watch party on Disney Plus, the following information is just for you. We've outlined a few simple steps to help you start and clarify the process.

  • Setting Up the Extension
      To get started:-
    • Firstly, install the Disney Plus Party extension by clicking here.
    • Ensure you have a compatible device, such as a Windows, macOS, or Chromebook laptop/PC.
    • Ensure you're using a supported browser like Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge to guarantee a seamless installation process.
  • Secure the Extension on Your Toolbar The next step is to pin the watch party extension on your device. Further, click on the extension icon in your browser's toolbar, then select the pin icon to ensure the Disney Plus Party Chrome extension is always easily accessible.
  • Log In to Disney Plus After pinning the extension, the next step is to log in to your Disney Plus account. Ensure you have an active Disney Plus subscription to use the extension. However, if you're new to Disney Plus, create an account first. Otherwise, proceed with the sign-in process to join or host a watch party.
  • Create Your Disney Plus Watch Party You'll set up your Disney Plus Watch Party in the fourth step. Afterwards, begin by logging into your Disney Plus account and selecting a video you want to watch with friends. Once the video plays, click the “DP” button on your browser toolbar. Then, choose the “Start the Party” option to create a watch party URL. Share this URL with your friends and loved ones to invite them to join the fun!
  • Join a Disney Plus Watch Party Wrap up your Disney Plus Party setup by joining your watch party with friends and loved ones. Begin by installing the Disney Plus Watch Party extension and logging into your Disney Plus account. Then, click on the watch link your friend or the host shared. Furthermore, this link instantly connects you to the watch party, setting the stage for a delightful shared streaming experience
